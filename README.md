# Real-world Data Project

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end analysis of a real-world Walmart retail transaction dataset using Python. The objective is to explore sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, product category trends, branch performance, and profitability through data analysis and visualization.

The project follows a complete data analytics workflow, including data cleaning, feature engineering, exploratory data analysis (EDA), KPI generation, and business recommendations.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze retail sales performance
- Identify high-performing branches and cities
- Evaluate product category sales and profit margins
- Understand customer purchasing behavior
- Analyze payment method preferences
- Explore monthly sales trends
- Generate actionable business insights and recommendations

---

## 📂 Dataset Information

- **Dataset:** Walmart Retail Transactions
- **Domain:** Retail Analytics
- **Records:** 10,051+ transactions

## 💡 Key Insights

- Product categories contribute differently to total sales and profitability.
- Sales performance varies across branches and cities.
- Customer ratings remain consistently positive across transactions.
- Certain payment methods are more frequently used than others.
- Monthly sales trends reveal fluctuations in customer demand.
- Profit margins differ significantly among product categories.

---

## ✅ Business Recommendations

- Increase inventory for high-performing product categories.
- Promote low-performing categories through targeted marketing campaigns.
- Improve inventory planning based on monthly sales trends.
- Encourage digital payment methods through promotional offers.
- Focus marketing efforts on cities with high growth potential.
- Optimize staffing during peak business hours.
